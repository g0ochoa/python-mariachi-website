Who We Are

Authentic Mariachi
Rooted in Tradition

Mariachi Todo Terreno was born from a deep love for the rich cultural heritage of Mexican music. Led by Gerry Ochoa, our ensemble has performed across California, delivering world-class mariachi entertainment to thousands of guests.

From heartfelt rancheras to romantic boleros, our musicians master every style. Each performance is tailored to your event — we arrive in traditional traje de charro, ready to make your celebration unforgettable.
